A high-intensity cardio workout designed to burn calories and improve cardiovascular fitness. Strength Training

Strength Training focuses on building muscle, increasing strength, and improving overall body composition. This class incorporates resistance exercises such as weightlifting, bodyweight exercises, and functional movements to target different muscle groups. By regularly participating in Strength Training, you can enhance your physical strength, boost metabolism, and promote bone density.

Yoga Flow

Yoga Flow is a class that emphasizes relaxation, stretching, and enhancing flexibility through various yoga poses and sequences. It combines gentle movements, controlled breathing techniques, and mindfulness to promote physical and mental well-being. Yoga Flow is suitable for individuals of all fitness levels and can help improve flexibility, balance, and stress reduction.
